## Q&A: Distribution Questions

### Q: Should I use email or Slack?
**A:** 
- **Email:** For critical, time-sensitive (pre-work, go/no-go decisions)
- **Slack:** For daily communication and team energy
- **Both:** For Phase 3 kickoff and retrospective

### Q: Is it okay to send everything Friday before?
**A:** No. Spacing out (Sat morning, Sun evening, Mon morning) gives people time to absorb and send help requests.

### Q: What if someone doesn't read the message?
**A:** They'll get it in standup. Help them catch up there. No judgment.

### Q: Should I post daily updates if there are no wins?
**A:** Yes. Even "we fixed 1 blocker" is a win. There's always something to celebrate.

### Q: Can I skip the retrospective?
**A:** No. Retrospectives are how you improve the framework for next team. Plus team deserves celebration.

### Q: What timezone should all times be?
**A:** PT (Pacific Time) for this team. Include note: "All times PT (Pacific Time). Convert yours here: https://timeanddate.com"
